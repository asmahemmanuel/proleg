About Us

Mr. Addo-Danquah Kesse Theophilus, a member of the Nsawam District Brigade Band started his music carrer as a trumpeter in the year 2013. He attended Ofori Panin and he was the leading trumpeter and band leader fast-forward. In the year 2018, he made a move to play the saxophone which was something he never envisaged. Being in the large market for four solid years, theosaxx developed the passion to raise upcoming youngsters in this field. Currently, his targeted audience include musicians, fellow instrumentalists and music lovers.

Available Services

MUSIC TUTORIALS

Instructional sessions designed to help individuals learn and develop thier musical skills. Topics include: Music theory, Instrument technique, Sight-reading, Improvisation and Composition.

SOLO SAX PERFORMANCE

Musical performance featuring the saxophone as the sole melodic instrument. Solo saxophone performances can encompass various genres, includung classical, jazz, pop, and experimental music.

BRIGADE CONDUCTING

Specialized form of conducting primarily used in marching bands, drum corps, and other large ensembles. Focuses on guiding and directing marching formations and choreographed movements while maintaining musical precision.

LIVE BAND PERFORMANCE

Musicians performing music together in real-time for a live audience. Live band can encompass various musical genres, such as rock, pop, jazz, blues, and reggae, and are often known for their vibrant energy and captivating stage presence.
